Donations

Donation Request

Please note the following before requesting donations.

Mandatory Criteria

Must be described as one of the following:

  • An organization holding a current tax-exempt status under Section 501(c)(3), (4), (6) or (19) of the Internal Revenue Code.
  • A recognized government entity: state, county, or city agency, including law enforcement or fire departments, that are requesting goods exclusively for public purposes.

Due to the nature of our products, we cannot donate to any school-related events or organizations.

Selection Process

  • We will review and make initial funding decisions on all requests submitted 30 days prior to the required donation date.
  • Applications are typically reviewed within 14 days of the submission date. Organizations will be notified of decisions via email or phone. All funding decisions are final.
  • Upon approval, donations may be picked up at our taproom during operating hours.

Due to a high frequency of donation requests, we can only make contributions to a select amount of request submissions.

Make an Official Donation Request

Please fill out the form below. Requests will be reviewed within 2–3 weeks and the contact person will be notified on approval.
