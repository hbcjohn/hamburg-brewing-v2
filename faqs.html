Taproom

What are your current hours?

Monday - Tuesday: 4:00PM - 10:00PM
Wednesday - Saturday: 12:00PM - 10:00PM
Sunday: 10:00AM - 8:00PM (Brunch 10am-12pm, Kitchen closed 12-12:30pm)

Do you take reservations?

We do not take reservations. All seating is first come first served.

Is the patio open?

Yes! Our patio is open seasonally. We are open for both indoor dining and outdoor seating on the patio.

I want to host an event. How do I book?

Interested in hosting your next event at Hamburg Brewing Company? From weddings to work parties, we've got you covered! Please fill out our Book Your Event form.

What is your Bus & Limo Policy?

Please review our full Bus & Limo Policy for details on large group transportation.

Are dogs allowed?

Dogs are welcome on our outdoor patio area. Please keep them leashed and ensure they are well-behaved around other guests.

Donations

Can I request a donation?

Yes! We are happy to consider donation requests for local community events and organizations. Please fill out our donation request form and we'll review your request.

The Beer

Where can I find your beer?

Our beers are available at the brewery taproom and at select bars, restaurants, and stores throughout Western New York. Check with your local retailer or visit us for the freshest selection.

I want to serve or sell Hamburg beer in my bar, store or restaurant. How do I do it?

We'd love to partner with you! Please fill out our New Account Inquiry form and our sales team will get in touch with you.
