About Us

Opened in October of 2013, Hamburg Brewing Company is a production brewing facility complete with an alluring taproom and patio. We invite you to check out our place in Hamburg, NY where you can relax, listen to live local music, and enjoy our great beers.

Our mission is simple: Provide high quality, innovative brews and have a good time doing it.
