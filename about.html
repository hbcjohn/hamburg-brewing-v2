About Us

Opened in October of 2013, Hamburg Brewing Company is a production brewing facility complete with an alluring taproom and patio. We invite you to check out our place — just 20 minutes outside of Buffalo, NY — in Hamburg, NY where you can relax, listen to some live local music, and of course, enjoy the great beers we have to offer.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: Provide high quality, innovative brews and have a good time doing it.

Look for us around town and remember: Beer so good, it's hard to drink responsibly, but we highly recommend it!

Taproom hours are listed on our homepage. We do not take reservations, all seating is first come first served.
